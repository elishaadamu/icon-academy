Our Facilities

The National Institute for Security Studies is located within the precincts of the serene lower Usuma Dam, in Bwari Area Council of the Federal Capital Territory, Abuja. It is surrounded by the beautiful Bwari hills beside which lies the Usuma dam. The institute boasts state of the art facilities which ensure course participants have a pleasurable stay throughout the duration of their course. Furthermore, these facilities are constantly renovated and expanded, while new ones are added in line with global standards.

Facilities

Lecture Venues

IIPPS Acadamy has two large capacity auditoriums that are equipped with state of the art audio/video equipment to cater for lectures and presentations.

Library

IIPPS Acadamy boasts a fully functional library equipped with relevant collections of books and study materials that can satisfy the needs of course Participants. The library which opens by 08:00 hours and closes at 22:00 hours, always has a library attendant available within these periods to assist those wishing to access the facility. The library can currently sit 35 readers at a time and the serenity of the environment makes it ideal for students to conduct research. The Institute seizes every opportunity to stock the library with current works/literature for the upliftment of academic study/research.
