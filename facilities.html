Facilities

Lecture Venues

Institute for Research and Security Management has two large capacity auditoriums that are equipped with state of the art audio/video equipment to cater for lectures and presentations.

Library

Institute for Research and Security Management boasts a fully functional library equipped with relevant collections of books and study materials that can satisfy the needs of course Participants. The library which opens by 08:00 hours and closes at 22:00 hours, always has a library attendant available within these periods to assist those wishing to access the facility. The library can currently sit 35 readers at a time and the serenity of the environment makes it ideal for students to conduct research. The Institute seizes every opportunity to stock the library with current works/literature for the upliftment of academic study/research.
