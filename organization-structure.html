Organisation Structure

BOARD OF GOVERNORS

The Board of Governors consists of a Chairman, the Director General of the Institute and nine other members including the secretary to the Director General of the Institute.

The board shall have power to:
  • Formulate policies and set targets for the Institute.
  • Determine and monitor programs to be run by the Institute.
  • Select candidates and institutions for course participation at the Institute.
  • Determine appropriate fees payable by external participants.
  • Set out guidelines for admission, criteria, and requirements for all courses and programs run by the Institute.
  • Approve the curricula of the Institute.
  • Consider and approve the appointment of the Director General of the Institute upon the recommendation of the Director-General.
  • Serve as the appellate body on disciplinary matters affecting staff of the Institute on grade level 8 and above.
  • Periodically review the performance of the Institute.
  • Guide the establishment of organizational structures and their duties.


The Director General

The Director General is appointed by the Director General of the Institute and responsible to the Board of Governors for the day to day management of the Institute. He is assisted in managing the daily affairs of the Institute by seven other Directors (the Director of Institute, Director Admin, Director of coordination and liaison services, Director Budget and Planning, Director Research, Evaluation and Library Services, Director Technical, the Secretary and DG)

The Director General shall:
  • Authorize programs and plans to give effect to policies approved by the Board subject to all or any of the powers in this section as may be delegated to him by the Board;
  • Exercise executive control over the staff of the Institute.
  • Determine and monitor programs to be run by the Institute;
  • Be responsible to the Board for the management of the affairs of the Institute;
  • Interpret policies and ancillary matters to other staff of the Institute; and
  • Ensure that the Institute attains and maintains a high academic standard of excellence.
