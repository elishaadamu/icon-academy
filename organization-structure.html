Organisation Structure

BOARD OF GOVERNORS

The Board of Governors consists of a Chairman, the Commandant of the Institute and five other members including the, Permanent secretary, Special Service Office, The National Security Adviser, Director-General State Service, Director-General Nigeria Intelligence Agency, Chief of Defence Intelligence, Director of administration who is secretary of the board.

The board shall have power to:
  • determine the mission, vision and purpose of the Institute;
  • formulate policies and set targets for the Institute;
  • determine and monitor programmes to be run by the Institute;.
  • select candidates and institutions for Course participation at the Institute;
  • determine and approve budgets, estimates and investment plans of the Institute;
  • determine appropriate fees payable by external participants;
  • set out guidelines for admission, criteria and requirements for all courses and programs run by the Institute;
  • approve the curricula of the Institute;
  • consider and approve the appointment of the Commandant of the Institute upon recommendation of the Director-General State Security Service;
  • serve as the appellate body on disciplinary matters affecting staff of the Institute on grade level 8 and above;
  • periodically review the performance of the Institute;
  • guide the establishment of organisational structures and their duties:

The Commandant

The Commandant of IIPPS Academy is appointed by the Director General, State Security Service and responsible to the Board for the day to day management of the Institute. He is assisted in managing the daily affairs of the Institute by five Directors (the Director of Research Estimate and Library Services, the Director of Studies, Director of Administration, Director of Technical Services, Director of coordination and liaison services and other Principal Officers who are answerable to him on matters concerning their respective Directorates and Departments.)
