Why Choose Us

Best Education

IRSM's purpose is to define the future of Professional security and Policy. We are guided by a distinct set of values—non-partisanship, independent thought, innovative thinking, cross-disciplinary sponsorship, integrity and professionalism, and talent development.

IRSM's values work in concert toward the goal of making real-world impact.

IRSM IMPACT

IRSM Students bring their Professional Security and policy expertise, judgment, and robust networks to their research, analysis, and recommendations.

We organize conferences, Seminars and workshop and make media appearances that aim to increase the knowledge, awareness, and salience of Profession Security and policy issues with relevant stakeholders and the interested public.
